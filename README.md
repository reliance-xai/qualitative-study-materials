This repository contains the materials developed and applied in a qualitative study to investigate the adoption of design artifacts to reason on reliance issues during the design of Interactive Explainable User Interfaces (XUIs).

The repository is organized as follows:
- Analysis Guiding Questions: Contains the proposed guiding questions to support the definition of Personas and Scenarios of Use by considering reliance issues through its impact factors;
- Persona and Scenario - Training: This is the Persona and Scenario of Use provided to the participants during the training activities of the qualitative study;
- Persona and Scenario - Application: This is the template for Persona dna Scenario of Use provided to the participants during the artifacts application of the qualitative study;
- Diagram - Training: Contains the MoLIC diagram that represents the Scenario of Use provided to the participants during the training activities;
- Diagram - Application: Contains the initial MoLIC diagram and the guidelines for the MoLIC notation elements provided to the participants during the artifacts application;
- Questionnaires and Interview: Contains the profile characterization questionnaire, the TAM adapted questionnaire, and the script for the semi-structured interview applied during our qualitative study;
- Training Slides: This is the material explained to the participants at the beggining of the study to provide the participants with the same extent of knowledge before starting the design tasks.
