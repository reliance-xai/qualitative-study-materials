This repository contains materials from a qualitative study that aims to investigate the adoption of design artifacts to reason on reliance issues during the design of Interactive Explainable User Interfaces (XUIs).

The repository is organized as follows:
- **Analysis Guiding Questions:** This file contains the proposed guiding questions that support defining personas and scenarios of use by considering reliance impact factors;
- **Persona and Scenario - Training:** This is the persona and scenario of use provided to participants during the training activities of the qualitative study;
- **Persona and Scenario - Application:** This is the template for persona and scenario of use provided to participants during the application of artifacts in the qualitative study;
- **Diagram - Training:** Contains the MoLIC diagram representing the scenario of use provided to participants during the training activities;
- **Diagram - Application:** Contains the initial MoLIC diagram and guidelines for MoLIC notation elements, which were provided to participants during the application of artifacts;
- **Questionnaires and Interview:** Contains the profile characterization questionnaire, the adapted TAM questionnaire, and the script for the semi-structured interview applied during the qualitative study;
- **Training Slides:** This material was explained to the participants at the beginning of the study to give them the same extent of knowledge before starting the design tasks.
